About Lahore

Nestled in the heart of Punjab, Lahore is known for its rich culture, scenic beauty, and warm hospitality. From historical sites to vibrant markets, this place has it all.

↓

Famous Places in Lahore

Badshahi Mosque

Built in 1673 by Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb, it's one of the largest mosques in the world.

Shalimar Gardens

Constructed in 1641 during Shah Jahan's reign, these gardens are a Mughal masterpiece.

Minar-e-Pakistan

Symbol of Pakistan’s independence, built on the site of the 1940 Lahore Resolution.

Lahore Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site with roots dating back to the 11th century.
